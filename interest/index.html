Interests

Research Interests:

The approximation theory of neural networks is interesting and technical. Unlike traditional tools to approximate functions, neural networks has two key benefits.
  • The architecture of neural networks is easy but powerful because of compositions. For example, if we use $f_2\circ f_1$ to approximate $f$, we only need to care the values of $f_2$ on $\text{Im}f_1$, which is extremely efficient when $\text{Im}f_1$ only contains finite points.
  • Neural networks is flexible. That is, neural networks can perform well even without the latent structures of the target functions.
